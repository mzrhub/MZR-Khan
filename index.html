Leadership & Vision

MZR KHAN is more than an entrepreneur; he's a movement. As Founder & CEO of MZR HUB (parent company), he oversees a diversified empire including MZR Life Essentials, MZR Tech, Rehman Online Quran Academy, and Zedemy. His journey from a classroom teacher to a digital pioneer defines resilience.
